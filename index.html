FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?

Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It’s suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

Yes, Frontend Mentor offers both free and premium challenges. You can access a wide range of free projects to practice your skills, and if you want to take it further, there are premium challenges available with more advanced designs and assets.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

Absolutely! Frontend Mentor encourages you to use the projects you build in your portfolio. It’s a great way to showcase your skills with real-world examples and demonstrate your ability to translate designs into functional code.

How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?

If you’re stuck, you can ask for help in the Frontend Mentor Discord community, check the solution discussions, or browse the forum. It's a supportive space full of developers of all levels who are happy to offer guidance.
